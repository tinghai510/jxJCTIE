Exhibition Overview

The theme of this exhibition is "Jiangxi's green travel arouses hearty 'bravo'", which is like a magnificent painting, slowly unfolding the unique charm and outstanding style of Jiangxi. The promotion event will take place in December 2024, while the official expo will be held in London, UK in September 2025.

The scale of the exhibition is grand and magnificent, with three comprehensive exhibition areas resembling three brilliant pearls. Each themed exhibition area has four special zones, which are finely divided according to the 11 prefecture level cities in Jiangxi. Each exhibition area is like a dreamlike realm, vividly depicting the local charm and rare treasures. For example, in the Nanchang exhibition area, the ancient Tengwang Pavilion stands tall like a historical giant, telling a millennium legend, while modern technological achievements are like brilliant stars, shining with innovative vitality; In the Jiujiang exhibition area, the magnificent scenery of Mount Lu resembles a painting of a fairy palace, the vast scenery of Poyang Lake resembles a dreamlike realm, and the abundant aquatic resources and characteristic agricultural products are like treasures gifted by nature, making one's mouth water; In the Jingdezhen exhibition area, exquisite ceramic artworks resemble an artistic starry sky, showcasing the extraordinary craftsmanship of the millennium porcelain capital from the ancient charm of traditional blue and white porcelain to the dynamic novelty of modern creative ceramics; In the Yingtan exhibition area, the mysterious Taoist culture sounds like ethereal sounds, and the unique mountains and beautiful waters of Longhu Mountain resemble a fairyland on earth.

This exhibition features over a thousand booths, arranged in an orderly manner like stars, showcasing unique products from various regions of Jiangxi. Exquisite handicrafts embody the spirit and hard work of craftsmen, green and ecological agricultural products emit the rich fragrance of nature, and innovative technological products showcase the vigorous development and progress of Jiangxi. Here, you can have in-depth exchanges and cooperation with enterprises in Jiangxi, work together to explore new vast horizons, and share development opportunities.

The positioning of this expo is to create an international, professional, and market-oriented platform for cultural tourism industry exchange and exhibition. It aims to promote the high-quality development of the cultural tourism industry, promote the deep integration of culture and tourism, and strengthen international technological and industrial cooperation.

The purpose of this exhibition is to attract foreign enterprises and consumers to the exhibition, successfully attract investment, and expand the foreign market; Promote economic integration and cultural exchange, and contribute to the early formation of a complete international exhibition industry chain in Jiangxi; Secondly, promote green tourism resources in Jiangxi, attract domestic and foreign tourists, and enhance the international reputation and influence of Jiangxi tourism; Finally, showcasing the green development achievements of Jiangxi in the field of cultural tourism, promoting the sustainable development of the cultural tourism industry, building an international exchange and cooperation platform, attracting domestic and foreign investment, promoting the upgrading and innovation of Jiangxi's cultural tourism industry, providing a display platform for exhibitors, expanding market channels, and enhancing brand awareness. And the exhibition industry chain is formed around the development of exhibition activities, with related enterprises relying on the exhibition industry to better promote the development of exhibition activities and obtain their own maximum benefits. Every node in the industrial chain pursues specialized and optimized development, and each industry is interrelated. The behavior of each node will affect the decisions of other enterprises in the chain. When developing the exhibition industry, it is necessary to comprehensively consider the integrity of the industrial chain.

And this exhibition will also serve as a brilliant bridge, connecting tourists and businesses from the UK and even the world, providing them with an excellent platform to deeply understand and enter Jiangxi. Here, you can not only appreciate the picturesque natural scenery of Jiangxi, but also appreciate the profound cultural landscape. You can also feel the positive exploration and innovative achievements of Jiangxi in green travel, ecological tourism, and other aspects. Whether hiking among the green mountains and waters of Jiangxi, listening to the beautiful melodies of nature, or taking eco-friendly transportation to experience the tranquility, peace, and picturesque beauty of the countryside, one can appreciate the unique charm of Jiangxi and immerse themselves in the endless charm of this magical land during the journey.

This Jiangxi Cultural Tourism International Expo is not only a visual, auditory, and gustatory feast, but also a platform for cultural exchange and cooperation. It showcases the unique charm of Jiangxi to the world and injects new vitality into promoting the development of Jiangxi's cultural tourism industry.
