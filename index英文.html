Exposition Introduction

Jiangxi has a profound historical and cultural heritage and beautiful natural scenery. This expo aims to showcase the unique cultural charm and rich tourism resources of Jiangxi to the world, and promote the international development of Jiangxi's cultural tourism industry. Showcasing the intangible cultural heritage of Jiangxi and inviting cultural tourism experts, scholars, and business representatives from both domestic and foreign countries to participate. Promoting international cultural and tourism exchanges and cooperation, and showcase Jiangxi's famous tourist attractions such as red culture, ceramic culture, and academy culture.

Pavilion Layout

“Green Rhyme Tour”

Hall 1

Green Tourism

Entering the Green Tourism Exposition Hall feels like stepping into a beautiful ecological painting. This showcases the wonderful achievements of the perfect integration of sustainable development concepts and tourism. Here, you can enjoy the primitive scenery within the nature reserve, from dense forests to clear streams, from towering mountains to vast grasslands, every scenery is enchanting.The display of characteristic ecological tourism scenic spots presents how to provide tourists with unique tourism experiences while protecting the natural environment.

“Green Delicacies Path”

Hall 2

Green Catering

The Green Catering Exposition Hall is a wonderful encounter between taste buds and environmental protection, where green cuisine from all over Jiangxi is gathered. From traditional specialty snacks to exquisite high-end cuisine, everything is available. There are also professional foodies on site to provide explanations and tasting activities, where you can taste the unique flavors of different regions in Jiangxi and learn about the food culture of various parts of Jiangxi. Whether it's sweet and delicious pastries, juicy fruits, flavorful meat products, or nutritious green foods, they can all satisfy your appetite.

“Green Energy Light”

Hall 3

Green Technology

Step into the Green Technology Exposition Hall, experience the perfect combination of technology and environmental protection, and witness the path of green transformation in technological development. The exhibition hall showcases various cutting-edge green technology achievements, including efficient utilization devices for new renewable energy technologies such as solar, wind, and hydro energy, energy-saving building materials, and design concepts. Show how to create more environmentally friendly and comfortable living and office spaces, as well as advanced resource recycling and reuse technologies.
